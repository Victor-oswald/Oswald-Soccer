AFC Bournemouth confirms Garyy O'Neil's dismissal

AFC Bournemouth has confirmed the dismissal of Garry O'Neil as the head coach.

After the cherries struggled to secure a place in the EPL just a season after gaining promotion, the team has announced parting ways with Garry as their head coach. He was the successor of Scott Parker and also part of parkers' coaching staffs who led the club to premier league promotion,Parker was sacked by the club on August 2022. Garry O'Neil as a head coach struggled in the bare bottom of the premier league table but fortunately escaped relegation from the league.

He took the Cherries through 34 premier league matches with ten wins,six draws, and 18 losses while serving as the cherries head coach.
