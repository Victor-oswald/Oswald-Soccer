Who is the best striker in England?

In the history of English football, certain strikers acquired just enough accolades to be named the best, not just in England but across the globe.

Though this might seem a bit unfair to compare legends with players who are still struggling with building their career stats and profile. They are some criteria which should justify the comparison. The players' all time stats relative to his achievements at certain level of team quality provides just enough information about his career rank.

We will be looking at some of the best English strikers with reference to their time of play,this will justify the fact that football has evolved so much in the later days.
