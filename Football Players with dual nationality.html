Football Players with dual nationality

Some players who changed their national teams might be due to dual citizenship by birth or by registration or otherwise.

Due to their right to choice of nation, they are allowed to migrate from one national team to another. Some of these players switch nations just to achieve their career ambition why some of them practically switch nations for personal reasons. Football Players are allowed to play for any nation provided that they meet the FIFA requirements to switch national team.

In 2004, FIFA rule made switching to any national team possible provided the player proves his connection with the nation he/she wishes to play for and has not featured in major competitions like the world cup tournament for his current nation.
