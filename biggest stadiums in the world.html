International Stadiums, Their Values And Their Capacities


The values and the capacities of different international stadiums varies, These factors are very essential as they determine the kind of competition to be held in the stadium in question.

While some stadiums can not hold a large number of people,hence can not accomodate a competition that requires a large capacity of persons, others are designed in such a way that they can handle a very large number of visitors. The capacity of a stadium is not just essential for the competition to be held, it is also very important for the owners of the team as it determines the revenue they generate from every competition being held in their stadium and also gives room for a very well equiped and standard training center for the players .

Some club owners invest wildly on stadiums in other to accomodate guests comfortably and also to realize bigger profit in their investments, therefore raising the value of their stadium to an enviable height. The values and the capacities of stadiums today depends on the competitions the team is involved in, this is simply because the competitions they are involved in determines the number or the kinds of guests and fans to be expected in every competition because the popular the competition, the bigger the guests to expect.
