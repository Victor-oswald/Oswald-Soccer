Some football Players who turned managers for the same club

Some players after retirement becomes managers or work as a management staff in the same club which they played in. This can be seen in most proffessional leagues, it could be because of the players relationship with the club or his level of play making skills on the pitch.

This incident is often seen in situations where the player has developed a long term relationship with the club, but sometimes it does not depend on any of the above factors, it's purely business.

These are some players who managed thesame club they played in:

Kenny being a Scottish player, played for Celtic in the late 60's and 70's before moving on to join the English side team "Liverpool". He became one of the most successful strikers in both Liverpool and Celtic.

    Kenny was contracted by Liverpool as their manager in 1985 to replace Fagan,his success story continued as he led Liverpool to winning several trophies and titles before before his resignation in 1991.   • Johan Cruyff:
