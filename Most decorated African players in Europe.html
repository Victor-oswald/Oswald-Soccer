The Most Award winning African footballers



Some African footballers has conquered many European competitions by lifting different trophies and holding different records for the teams they played in.

With the membership of almost all the African nations in the FIFA community, African football has earned global recognition hence allowing their players to take part in the world football. African players are found in all European major soccer leagues as they have become the bedrock of some teams in Europe.

Some Of The Most Decorated African Players In Major European leagues


Mikel Obi:

Mikel John Obi was born on 22nd April, 1987 in Nigeria. He played as a defensive Midfielder for European clubs. Mikel Obi played for the turkish league and premier league clubs, He spent most of his carer with the Chelsea football club since he joined them in 2006. He left the club in 2017 making a total of 249 appearances for the with a single goal.

Augustine Azu:

Popularly known by his nickname Jay jay Okocha, was born on August, 1973 in Nigeria. He started his proffessional footballing career from Enugu Rangers in the Nigerian Proffessional football league before joining a team in the German Oberliga west after which he joned Eintracht Frankfurt in the Germany bundesliga in 1992. He advanced his career to play in clubs including Turkish club, Paris Saint Germain,English clubs and Arabian club. Okocha made a total of 454 appearances playing as a Midfielder. He scored a total of 89 career goals.

Didier Drogba:

The Ivorian is known for his records in the European football. He was born on march 11th, 1978 in Abidjan, Ivory Coast. He played as a striker in several European clubs but he spent most of his career playing for Chelsea FC in the English Premier league. He made a total of 226 appearances for Chelsea and scored a total of 100 goals.

Mohamed Salah:

Salah is a very important personality in the European football, he was born on 15th June, 1992 in Basyoun, Egypt. He currently plays for English Premier league as a forward since 2017. Salah has remained one of the top goal scorers in the club history with a total of 137 goals in 217 appearances.

Yaya Toure:

Gnegneri Yaya Toure was an Ivorian professional footballer who played as a Midfielder for European clubs including Manchester City, Monaco, Barcelona and others. Yaya Toure currently works as a coach for the Tottenham Hotspur academy team. He is considered one of the best footballers of all time in Africa. He won the African cup of nation's in 2015 with the Ivory coast national team. He made a total of 470 career appearances, scoring a total of 79 career goals.

Nwankwo Kanu:

Kanu Nwankwo is another African player who have played a very important role in the European football competitions. He was born on August, 1979 in Nigeria, he played as a forward for many European teams including Ajax, Inter Milan, Arsenal, WestBromwich Albion amongst others. He holds several titles in Europe, including UEFA Champions league medal, three different FA Cup medals, UEFA Cup medal and Olympic gold medal. He made a total of 406 career appearances with 98 goals.
