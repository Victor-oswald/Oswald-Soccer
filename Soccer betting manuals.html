Soccer Betting Terms

What is 1 X 2

This is the term used in Soccer betting to represent the team that will win in the the regular time(extra time not included otherwise specified. ).
see breakdown below:
1: This implies that the team at home will win the game in the regular time.

x: This implies that that the two teams will finish draw in the regular time i.e no one wins the game.

2: This implies that the visiting team will win the game in the regular time.
