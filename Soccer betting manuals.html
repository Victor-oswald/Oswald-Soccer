Soccer Betting Terms
please note that the regular time in a match is 90 minutes i.e extra time is not inclusive.

What is 1 X 2

This is the term used in Soccer betting to represent the team that will win in the the regular time(extra time not included otherwise specified. ).

see breakdown below:

1: This implies that the team at home will win the game in the regular time. It is called home.

x: This implies that that the two teams will finish draw in the regular time i.e no one wins the game. It is called draw

2: This implies that the visiting team will win the game in the regular time. It is called away

What is GG/NG

This term is used in Soccer betting to represent either of the teams or neither of them or both of them to score in the match during the regular time.

see breakdown below

GG: This implies that the both teams will score goal(s) in the regular time. It is called Goal-Goal.

NG: This implies that either of the teams will score or neither of them will score in the regular time. It is called No-Goal.

What is ODD/EVEN

This term is used in Soccer betting to represent whether the total goal(s) scored in the regular time of the match equals an even or odd number.

See breakdown below

EVEN: This implies that the total goal(s) scored in the regular time of the match equals an even number.

ODD: This implies that the total goal(s) scored in the regular time of the match equals an odd number.

Halftime Events

A regular match is divided into two halves including the 'first' and the 'second'
This term is used in soccer betting to represent all events in either the first or the second half.

See breakdown below

First/Second Half 1 x 2: This is used to represent either of the team to win the match or the both teams play draw in the specified half.

First/Second Half O/U: This is used to represent whether the total goal(s) scored in the selected half is above or below(to be specified) the specified number.
