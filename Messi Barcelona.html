Trophies and titles

Messi's career in Barcelona is what most people see as a completed or fulfilled Career. After his debut match for the club in 2004, he has remained the club's top goal scorer of all time having captained the club to several competition victories since 2018. He is also the highest goal scorer for a single club after breaking pele's record. He holds a record of 672 goals for Barcelona and won his first Ballon d'or when he was 22 years old. Messi finished his stay in Barcelona with a total of 7 ballon d'ors out of which he won four consecutively. He also won six golden boots in Barcelona. Messi also recorded three champions league titles, seven copa del rey and a total of ten Laliga titles.
