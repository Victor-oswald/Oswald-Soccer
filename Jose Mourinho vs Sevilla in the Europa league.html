Sevilla vs Roma: History broken


The Europa league final between Sevilla and Roma leaves everyone surprised as Jose mourinho's record of winning every Europa final he plays was broken by Sevilla.

the saga got more interesting as Sevilla's victory features two Moroccan national team members Yassin Bono and Youssef El-Nesiri who also featured in the Morocco 1-nill victory over Portugal in the 2022 FIFA world cup in Qatar helped Sevilla secure their seventh time Europa league title. Jose mourinho has won every single Europa competition final he has played including the Europa conference league final in his first season with Roma. He coached Roma through to the finals after maintaining a 1-nill aggregate over Bayer Leverkusen in the semi-finals while Sevilla beat Juventus in the semi-finals to make it to the final.

Sevilla defeated Roma even after Roma seemed to have a very good start in the early minutes of the game.
Alex Telles who also featured in the Sevilla's squad against Roma, helped knock Manchester United out in the quarter finals of the league despite being loaned to Roma from Manchester United in season's beginning.

The story becomes more intriguing knowing that Sevilla is in the bare bottom of the laliga standings for the just concluded laliga season. they didn't qualify for the Champions league in the forth coming season and barely qaulified for any European competition in the coming season.
This is still unbelievable to some people that Sevilla could lift the Europa league title despite competing with some major European clubs like Juventus, Manchester united, Barcelona and whole lot of others.

Bryan Gill also featured in the sevilla squad winning the Europa league title with Sevilla for the second time. He was part of the Sevilla's squad who won 2019-2020 Europa league title before he signed for Tottenham Hotspur in 2021. Back again to Sevilla,he won the Europa league title for the second time.
